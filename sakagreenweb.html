Our Recycling Process

Transparency in how we handle your donations

From Your Closet to New Products

When you donate clothes to SakaGreen, they embark on a sustainable journey:

  1. Collection:Clothes are received at our centers and sorted by condition.
  2. Donation:Wearable items are cleaned and donated to communities in need.
  3. Recycling:Non-wearable clothes are shredded and processed into fibers.
  4. Repurposing:Fibers become stuffing for pillows or materials for new products.

Track your donation's journey through our app and see the impact you're making!
